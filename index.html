About Divya

The Mirror, Reflects My Light

I am a lover of nature, drawn to tranquil places where the sky meets lush greenery, and the clouds create a sense of calm. I find peace in these serene landscapes, often reflecting on them as I lose myself in love story novels.

Music is my constant companion, whether it’s the deep, atmospheric beats of Alan Walker, the heartfelt songs of Justin Bieber and Conan Gray, or the soulful tunes of Arijit Singh, Darshan Raval, and Atif Aslam. In my free moments, I turn to art, where I can pour out my emotions, believing that if I could say it in words, there would be no need to paint.
